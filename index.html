About the course

01.

Why Winter
Consulting?

With countless courses online, navigating non-tech fields like consulting and product management can be challenging. Winter Consulting offers a focused 6-week program designed to cut through the noise and build a strong foundation in both domains.

02.

What does it
cover?

Our 6-week intensive program covers the core principles of consulting and product management, offering a clear, structured path to master the essentials and gain practical, industry-relevant skills.

03.

Who are we?

We are the Consulting & Analytics Club, IIT Guwahati, conducting this flagship Winter Consulting program each year. Initially created to train IIT Guwahati students, the course now reaches 25K+ learners across 35+ countries.
